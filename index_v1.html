MEET THE DESIGNER

Name:Hector Gatti

  • An Argentinian graphic designer and typographer
  • Runs Omnibus Type and Gatti Studio
  • In 2014, he designed Archivo
  • Omnibus Type, established in 2011, is a cooperative focused on web typography and the creation of high-quality web fonts.

ABOUT ARCHIVO

  • a grotesque font reminiscent of late nineteenth century
  • Sans-serif typeface
  • derived from CHIVO
  • It includes Normal, Black and Narrow styles
  • supports over 200 world languages
  • Archivo won an award at Tipos Latinos 2016, which is a Biannual Latin American typeface design competition
